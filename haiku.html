Haiku

Profile

My heart is aching
Like a lost soul wandering
Hollow and empty

My heart is broken
Like brittle rocks on a steep cliff
Defenseless fortress

My head boils over
As a volcano erupts
Like burning sulfur

Shadows of the night
Burning in my soul to die
Sleeping in despair

Castle on a hill
Are like trees near a river
Tall, strong and sturdy

Don't care anymore
When they all got someone else
You'll just die alone

On a cold dark night
I wonder about the stars
Where will we go next?
